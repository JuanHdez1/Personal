Who is Juan F. Hernandez

A GUY, A COMPUTER AND A PURPOSE

My name is Juan F. Hernández José, I am a first-generation afro-Latino American university student who grew up with technology in my surroundings and in my body. I consider myself a young adult who has initiative and is responsible and creative, with a strong desire to help others. My family is from the Dominican Republic, but I was raised in Puerto Rico. When I was two years old I was diagnosed with type 1 diabetes and had to learn to live with it. Ever since then, I adapted to living with this condition and leveraged the advances technology has given me. When I turned 8 I started relying on an insulin pump to maintain my blood sugar levels in control. Now I have learned to use technology in my favor to maintain a stable health. I have always wanted to create a device or computer program that can be used to improve the lifestyle of patients with diabetes and other conditions. In high school I participated in multiple coding workshops and seminars that encouraged me to pursue a career in programming. Therefore, I made the decision to study computer science at the University of Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras campus. There, I found that my strong capabilities in oratory, science, and math, proved to be great skills to help me succeed in my interests in coding and helping others. One of my main career goals is to have an impact on patients with compromised health conditions and develop unique solutions to improve their health and lifestyles. I am certain the knowledge as a programmer focused on the bioinformatics field, I can achieve my goal.
